The history of the steam engine

This is a picture of a US destroyer in WW2 and it uses a steam engine to move in battle.

Edward Huber was the man who invented the steam engine. He was a man of great vision. He could visualize how steam might displace the power of horses. His vision was realized through the development of the “The New Huber,” a steam powered engine built in 1878. The steam engine was very important as it could help people to travel faster instead of depending on horses to help them travel. The steam engine changed the world as many wanted it which it would be made into cars and trains. The steam engine helps the transport to move when you make it to move on its controls. It is a bit important as it help people to transport or travel as it would be much easier.
